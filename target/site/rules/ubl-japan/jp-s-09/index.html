jp-s-09

Message

The Consumption Tax category tax amount (BT-117) in a Consumption Tax breakdown (BG-23) where Consumption Tax category code (BT-118) is "Standard rated" shall equal the Consumption Tax category taxable amount (BT-116) multiplied by the Consumption Tax category rate (BT-119).

Context

(abs(xs:decimal(../cbc:TaxAmount)) - 1 < round((abs(xs:decimal(../cbc:TaxableAmount)) * (xs:decimal(cbc:Percent) div 100)) * 10 * 10) div 100 ) and (abs(xs:decimal(../cbc:TaxAmount)) + 1 > round((abs(xs:decimal(../cbc:TaxableAmount)) * (xs:decimal(cbc:Percent) div 100)) * 10 * 10) div 100 )
