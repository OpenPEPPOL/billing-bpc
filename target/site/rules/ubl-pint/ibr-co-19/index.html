ibr-co-19

Message

If Invoicing period (ibg-14) is used, the Invoicing period start date (ibt-073) or the Invoicing period end date (ibt-074) shall be filled, or both.

Context
cac:InvoicePeriod
Test
exists(cbc:StartDate) or exists(cbc:EndDate) or (exists(cbc:DescriptionCode) and not(exists(cbc:StartDate)) and not(exists(cbc:EndDate)))
